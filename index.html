Privacy Policy — Neural Forge

Effective date: 9 July 2026
App: Neural Forge (com.neuralforge.app)
Developer: The Fearne of Life
Contact: thefearneoflife@gmail.com

Neural Forge does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal information. The app works entirely offline. Everything you enter or generate — your profile, progress, journal, scores, and rewards — is stored only on your own device and never leaves it. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

What information the app stores (on your device only)

Neural Forge saves the following on your phone so the app can remember your progress between sessions. All of it lives in your device's private app storage and is accessible only to Neural Forge:

  • Profile details you choose to enter — such as your name, body metrics, fitness level, goals, and dietary preferences.
  • Programme progress — completed workouts, current week, streak, points, level, and Sparks balance.
  • Journal entries — mood, mental clarity, sleep hours, and any notes you write. These are private to you.
  • Game scores — your best results in the brain-training games.
  • Rewards — badges, titles, stickers, and themes you've earned or unlocked.
  • Step count — if you enable step tracking, a daily step total derived from your phone's built-in step sensor.
  • Settings — your preferences, including any emergency contact number you choose to set for the SOS feature, and your reminder settings.

We cannot see any of this information. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Information we do NOT collect

To be completely clear, Neural Forge does not:

  • Require an account or login.
  • Connect to the internet to send or receive your data.
  • Collect analytics or usage statistics.
  • Contain advertising or advertising identifiers.
  • Include third-party trackers or SDKs that gather data.
  • Access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or precise location.
  • Sell or share your information with anyone.

Permissions the app requests, and why

Neural Forge requests only two optional permissions, and only for the features that need them:

  • Physical activity / activity recognition — used solely to read your phone's built-in step counter so the app can show your daily steps. The data stays on your device. If you deny this permission, the rest of the app works normally; step tracking simply shows zero.
  • Notifications — used only to send you an optional local reminder to keep your streak going. These reminders are generated on your device; nothing is sent to or from a server. You can turn reminders off at any time in Settings.

The SOS / emergency feature opens your phone's own dialer pre-filled with an emergency number you choose. Neural Forge never places a call itself — you always decide whether to dial. No call information is recorded or transmitted.

Children's privacy

Neural Forge is intended for a general adult audience and does not knowingly collect information from children. Because the app collects no personal information at all, no data about any user — child or adult — is gathered or transmitted.

Data security

Because your information never leaves your device, there is no cloud database to breach. Your data is protected by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, and Android's per-app sandboxing). If you uninstall the app, its locally stored data is removed by the operating system. If you use Android's backup feature, your app data may be included in your own device backup under your Google account's control — this is handled by Android, not by Neural Forge.

Health and medical disclaimer

Neural Forge is a wellness and educational tool. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or condition. Exercise, nutrition, and supplement information in the app is general guidance only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any new exercise, diet, or supplement routine — especially if you take medication, are pregnant, or have a health condition.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same location as this document and the effective date above will be revised. Because the app collects no data, any change will relate to how the app behaves, not to how your information is used.
